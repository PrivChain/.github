# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community across all PrivChain repositories.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give constructive, technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus on project outcomes over personal disputes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or abuse

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct issues to:

- `hello@privchain.io`
- Subject: `Code of Conduct Report`

## Attribution

This policy is informed by Contributor Covenant principles and adapted for PrivChain.
